Photography

I adeptly handle seven different photographic styles—including product, fashion, long exposure, portraits, landscapes, wildlife, and action pictures—all while capturing moments that go beyond the usual and shed light on distinctive viewpoints. I have an intuitive eye for detail and am proficient in Adobe Lightroom. My artistic ability combines compositional brilliance with the technical dexterity of using a DSLR to create visually compelling and inspirational storytelling.

Graphic Designs

I am an expert in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, and I use my imagination to create visually striking and inventive designs by skillfully utilizing a variety of tools, such as 3D rendering, the accuracy of the pen tool, and the fluidity of the warp tool.

Cinematography

I have a strong sense of storytelling and am skilled with the Adobe Suite, especially Rush and Premiere Pro. I use these skills to craft complex stories into engaging documentaries and movies. Through the use of mood boards, storyboards, and a variety of lighting, audio, and filmmaking tools, I create immersive visual experiences by bringing the imagination to life.

This silent film, The Art of Moving On, tells a story of a young woman experiencing heartbreak. This documentary aims to express a storyline in two emotions—freedom and loneliness—simply through a camera lens.

This documentary, Tuning Dreams, tells a story of a recent graduate student fighting for his dreams. This documentary aims to reveal the strenuous days that come with making music and being unsure of what the future holds.
